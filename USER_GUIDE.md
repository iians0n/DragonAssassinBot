# 🎯 Assassins Game: Player Handbook

Welcome to the Assassins Game! This guide will teach you everything you need to know to survive, thrive, and rack up points for your team using the **Assassins Game Tracker Bot**.

---

## 🚀 Getting Started

All interactions with the bot MUST be done through **Direct Messages (DMs)**. Do not send commands in the main group chat!

1. Open a chat with the bot on Telegram.
2. Type `/start` to begin.
3. Tap **"📝 Register Now"** or type `/register`.
4. The bot will ask for your name and gender. 
5. Once you complete registration, the admins will assign you to a team, or you will be auto-assigned.

*Note: You cannot play or see your targets until you are assigned to a Team!*

---

## 🎮 How to Play

The game runs for **one week**, active daily between **9:00 AM and 11:00 PM SGT**. Outside of these hours, you are safe and cannot be killed.

Your goal is to eliminate players on opposing teams to earn points. You can check who is alive and on an opposing team by typing:
👉 `/targets`

### ⚔️ Types of Kills

There are two ways to eliminate a target. **You are limited to 2 kills per day.**

**1. Ping Pong Ball Kill (Normal)**
- **How:** Hit your target with a ping pong ball.
- **Report it:** Type `/ball @username` in the bot DM.
- **Points:** +10 Points
- **Penalty:** If you are killed this way, you are "dead" for **2 hours**.

**2. Post-It Note Kill (Stealth)**
- **How:** Stick a post-it note on your target's back without them noticing. **(Must be same gender)**
- **Report it:** Type `/postit @username`. *You MUST attach or reply with a photo of the post-it on the target's back!*
- **Points:** +5 Points
- **Penalty:** If you are killed this way, you are "dead" for **1 hour**.

### 💀 Death and Cooldowns

If someone reports a kill on you, you will receive a DM from the bot. 
- You can **Accept** the kill, and your cooldown will start immediately.
- If you believe the kill was unfair or against the rules, you can **Dispute** it. Provide a reason, and the Game Admins will review it.
- If you don't respond within 15 minutes, the kill is auto-accepted.

While you are "Dead" (on cooldown):
- ❌ You **CANNOT** eliminate other players.
- 🔴 You **CAN** still be eliminated by other players! (Stay alert!)

## 🎭 Hidden Roles

Every day, players are randomly secretly assigned special roles within their team. **Roles are hidden**, meaning the global leaderboard won't show them! 

- 👤 **Normal**: Regular player, no bonuses.
- 🥷 **Ninja**: Earns **double points** (+5 bonus) for successful stealth/post-it kills!
- 🎯 **Sniper**: Earns **double points** (+10 bonus) for successful ping pong ball kills!
- 👑 **President**: Very valuable target! If an opponent kills you, they earn **+50 bonus points**. If you die, you lose the President role and become a Normal player.

*Check your team's private group chat at the end of each day to see tomorrow's roles!*

---

## 🏆 Statistics and Commands

Keep track of your standing and your team's performance!

- `/profile` - View your total points, kills, deaths, KDA, and current kill streak.
- `/leaderboard` - View the Top 10 individual players and the overall Team Rankings.
- `/team` - View the total points and members of your specific team.
- `/stats @username` - Spy on the detailed stats of any specific player.
- `/countdown` - See exactly how much time is left in the game day or the entire week.
- `/achievements` - View badges you've unlocked (e.g., *First Blood, Penta Kill, Silent Assassin*).

### 🔥 Kill Streaks
Stringing together kills without dying will unlock public streak announcements in the main group chat, ranging from a **Triple Kill** all the way to **GODLIKE** (15 kills)!

Good luck, trust no one, and watch your back! 🎯
